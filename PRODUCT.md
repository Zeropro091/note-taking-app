# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Knowledge workers, researchers, and creators who need a structured, interconnected space for deep thought and project scaffolding.

## Product Purpose
A high-end, graph-based note-taking application that serves as a "second brain." It is optimized for both human readability and AI retrieval (RAG-ready semantic search).

## Brand Personality
Editorial, intelligent, restrained, and precise. It feels like a high-quality print magazine mixed with a powerful terminal.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered SaaS dashboards
- Neon gradients or "gamer" aesthetics
- Generic "startup" clean (boring)
- Overwhelming toolbars
- Nested cards

## Design Principles
- **Content is the Interface**: The notes are the hero. UI recedes until needed.
- **Print Meets Digital**: Warmth of paper, sharpness of ink, precision of code.
- **Quiet Power**: Advanced AI features (semantic search, auto-linking) are discoverable but never intrusive.
- **Rhythm and Contrast**: High typographic contrast to establish clear hierarchy without relying on boxes or cards.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA compliance.
- High contrast text.
- Keyboard navigable graph and editor.
