# Video Editing Service

**Category:** Digital Service
**Startup Cost:** Low ($100-500)
**Time to Profit:** 2-4 months
**Difficulty:** Medium

## Description
Edit videos for YouTubers, businesses, and content creators. High demand with short-form content boom.

## Services to Offer
- YouTube video editing
- Short-form content (Reels, TikTok, Shorts)
- Wedding/event videos
- Real estate video editing
- Corporate videos
- Motion graphics

## Revenue Model
- YouTube: $200-800 per video
- Short-form: $50-200 per video
- Packages: $1,000-3,000/month
- Weddings: $500-2,000 per event
- Retainers are key for stability

## Tools Needed
- Adobe Premiere Pro / Final Cut
- After Effects (for motion graphics)
- Stock footage/music subscriptions
- Fast computer for rendering

## Getting Started
1. Master one type of editing
2. Create portfolio with sample edits
3. Outreach to YouTubers in your niche
4. Offer free trial edit
5. Join Facebook groups for creators
6. Build systems for speed (templates)
