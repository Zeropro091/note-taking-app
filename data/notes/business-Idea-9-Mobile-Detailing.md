# Mobile Car Detailing

**Category:** Local Service
**Startup Cost:** Low ($500-2,000)
**Time to Profit:** 1-2 months
**Difficulty:** Low

## Description
Professional car detailing that comes to the customer's location (home, office).

## Services to Offer
- Basic wash and vacuum ($50-80)
- Interior detail ($100-150)
- Exterior detail ($100-150)
- Full detail ($175-300)
- Paint correction ($300-500)
- Ceramic coating ($300-600)

## Equipment Needed
- Portable water and power
- Pressure washer
- Vacuum and extractor
- Polisher (for paint correction)
- Cleaning chemicals
- Quality microfibers and towels
- Insurance

## Revenue Model
- 2-4 cars per day realistic
- Average ticket: $150-200
- Daily revenue: $300-600
- Monthly revenue: $6,000-12,000
- Seasonal: Better in spring/summer

## Getting Started
1. Start with basic package, add services
2. Focus on residential and office parks
5. Subscription model: Monthly maintenance
6. Bundle packages for better value
7. Referrals are key - offer discounts
