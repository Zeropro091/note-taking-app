# Print-on-Demand Brand

**Category:** E-commerce
**Startup Cost:** Very Low ($0-100)
**Time to Profit:** 3-6 months
**Difficulty:** Low

## Description
Design and sell custom merchandise without inventory. Products are printed and shipped when ordered.

## Products to Sell
- T-shirts and hoodies
- Mugs and tumblers
- Phone cases
- Wall art and posters
- Stickers
- Hats and bags

## Platforms
- Printful + Shopify
- Printify + Etsy
- Redbubble (marketplace)
- Amazon Merch (no marketing needed)

## Success Factors
- Specific niche targeting (not generic designs)
- Trend-aware designs
- Consistent new designs
- SEO and social media marketing
- Building a brand, not just products

## Revenue Model
- Design once, sell forever
- Profit per item: $5-15
- Target volume: 10-50 sales/day
- Scale with more designs and platforms

## Getting Started
1. Choose a passionate niche
2. Research trending designs in niche
3. Create 10-50 initial designs
4. Set up on 2-3 platforms
5. Promote on Pinterest, Instagram, TikTok
6. Analyze what sells, create more
