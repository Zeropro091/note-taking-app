# Online Tutoring Business

**Category:** Education
**Startup Cost:** Very Low ($0-100)
**Time to Profit:** Immediate
**Difficulty:** Low

## Description
Teach what you know online. One-on-one or small group tutoring via video call.

## Subjects in Demand
- English conversation (ESL)
- Math (K-12, test prep)
- Coding (web dev, Python)
- Music (guitar, piano)
- Test preparation (SAT, GRE, IELTS)
- Business skills

## Platforms to Use
- Start independent (Zoom + scheduling tool)
- Preply, Italki, Cambly (for traffic)
- Create your own booking site

## Revenue Model
- Independent: $30-100/hour (keep 100%)
- Platforms: $15-40/hour (platform takes 20-40%)
- Group classes: $20-40/student per hour
- Pre-recorded courses: Passive income

## Getting Started
1. Identify your teachable skills
2. Create simple website with Calendly booking
3. Offer free 15-min consultation
4. Get testimonials from first students
5. Increase rates as you build reputation
6. Create course bundles for passive income
