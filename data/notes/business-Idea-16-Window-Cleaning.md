# Window Cleaning Business

**Category:** Local Service
**Startup Cost:** Low ($200-1,000)
**Time to Profit:** 1-2 months
**Difficulty:** Low

## Description
Professional window cleaning for homes and businesses. Recurring revenue with regular service.

## Services to Offer
- Residential window cleaning
- Commercial storefront cleaning
- High-rise/window washing (certification needed)
- Solar panel cleaning
- Gutter cleaning (add-on)
- Pressure washing (add-on)

## Revenue Model
- Residential: $150-400 per home
- Commercial: $50-150 per visit
- Monthly routes: Stable recurring revenue
- Route optimization = more jobs per day

## Equipment Needed
- Squeegees and scrubbers
- Extension poles
- Water-fed pole system (for 3+ stories)
- Bucket and belts
- Transportation
- Insurance

## Getting Started
1. Start with residential (easier)
2. Offer discounts for first-time customers
3. Build monthly routes for efficiency
4. Commercial contracts are gold mines
5. Cross-sell gutter cleaning, pressure washing
6. Hire help as route grows
