# Virtual Assistant Agency

**Category:** B2B Service
**Startup Cost:** Very Low ($0-200)
**Time to Profit:** 1-2 months
**Difficulty:** Low

## Description
Provide remote administrative support to entrepreneurs and small businesses. Can scale into an agency.

## Services to Offer
- Email management and response
- Calendar and scheduling
- Data entry and research
- Social media posting
- Customer support
- Bookkeeping basics
- Project management

## Revenue Model
- Hourly: $20-50/hour
- Retainer packages: $500-2,000/month
- Agency model: Hire at $12-18/hr, bill $30-50/hr
- Niche VAs charge more (real estate, medical, legal)

## Getting Started
1. Identify your skills and services
2. Create packages (not hourly)
3. Network in entrepreneur Facebook groups
4. Cold email small business owners
5. Start with a few clients, hire help
6. Build agency as demand increases

## Scaling to Agency
1. Hire first VA when at capacity
2. Create SOPs for all tasks
3. Focus on sales and VA management
4. Niche down for higher rates
5. Add specialized services
