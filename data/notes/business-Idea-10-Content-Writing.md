# Freelance Content Writing

**Category:** Digital Service
**Startup Cost:** Very Low ($0-100)
**Time to Profit:** 1-2 months
**Difficulty:** Low

## Description
Write content for businesses on a freelance basis. Blog posts, website copy, emails, and more.

## Services to Offer
- Blog posts and articles
- Website copywriting
- Product descriptions
- Email sequences
- Video scripts
- Case studies
- Press releases

## Pricing Guide
- Blog posts: $100-500 each
- Website pages: $200-800 each
- Email sequences: $300-1,500
- Retainers: $2,000-5,000/month
- Charge by project, not hourly

## Finding Clients
- Content marketing agencies (outsource partner)
- Direct business outreach
- Upwork/Fiverr to start
- LinkedIn networking
- Cold email with samples

## Getting Started
1. Create 5-10 portfolio pieces (even if fake)
2. Pick a niche to specialize in
3. Create simple website with samples
4. Outreach to 20 businesses daily
5. Start with lower rates, increase quickly
6. Aim for retainer clients for stability
