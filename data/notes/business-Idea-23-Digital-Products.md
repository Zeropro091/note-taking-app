# Digital Products Business

**Category:** Passive Income
**Startup Cost:** Very Low ($0-200)
**Time to Profit:** 3-6 months
**Difficulty:** Medium

## Description
Create and sell digital products once, sell forever. True passive income potential.

## Product Ideas
- Ebooks and guides
- Templates (Notion, Excel, Canva)
- Presets and filters (Lightroom, video)
- Digital art and clipart
- Printables (planners, checklists)
- Courses (mini-courses)
- Software and tools

## Sales Channels
- Etsy (templates, printables)
- Gumroad (all digital products)
- Teachers Pay Teachers (educational)
- Your own site (Shopify, WooCommerce)
- Creative Market (design assets)

## Revenue Model
- Products: $5-100 each
- Bundle for higher average order
- Focus on volume over high prices
- Email list = repeat customers

## Getting Started
1. Solve a specific problem you have
2. Create the product
3. Set up on multiple marketplaces
4. Create content marketing the product
5. Build email list for launches
6. Expand product line over time
