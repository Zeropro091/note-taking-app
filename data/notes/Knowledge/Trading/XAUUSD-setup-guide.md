# 🥇 XAU/USD Trading Setup Guide

> Gold trading strategies, setups, and best practices.

---

## 📊 Market Overview

**XAU/USD** = Gold vs US Dollar

**Why Trade Gold?**
- High volatility = opportunity
- Strong trends
- Reacts to news predictably
- Good for technical analysis

## 🎯 Entry Strategies

### Strategy 1: Breakout Pullback

1. Wait for breakout of key level
2. Wait for pullback to broken level
3. Enter on rejection candle
4. SL below pullback low
5. TP at next key level

**Win Rate**: ~65%
**R:R**: 1:2 or better

### Strategy 2: Trend Continuation

1. Identify trend (H4 and H1 same direction)
2. Wait for pullback to 20/50 EMA
3. Enter on confirmation candle
4. SL below swing low/high
5. TP at next structure level

**Win Rate**: ~60%
**R:R**: 1:3 possible

## 📰 News That Moves Gold

| Event | Impact | Expected Move |
|-------|--------|---------------|
| FOMC | High | Rate hike = gold down |
| CPI | High | High inflation = gold up |
| NFP | High | Strong jobs = dollar up = gold down |
| Geopolitics | High | Tension = gold up (safe haven) |
| Fed Chair Speech | Medium | Hawkish = gold down |

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

1. **Trading during low volatility** (Asian session)
2. **No stop loss** - Gold can move $50+ in minutes
3. **Overleveraging** - Gold swings are wild
4. **Ignoring news** - NFP/FOMC can spike $100+
5. **Counter-trend trading** - Trends are strong in gold
