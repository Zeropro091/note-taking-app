# Pressure Washing Service

**Category:** Local Service
**Startup Cost:** Medium ($2,000-5,000)
**Time to Profit:** 1-2 months
**Difficulty:** Low

## Description
Exterior cleaning service for homes and businesses using pressure washing equipment.

## Services to Offer
- Driveway and sidewalk cleaning
- House exterior washing
- Deck and patio cleaning
- Fence cleaning
- Commercial parking lots
- Roof soft washing (specialized)

## Equipment Needed
- Commercial pressure washer ($1,000-3,000)
- Surface cleaner attachment
- Chemicals and cleaning solutions
- Transportation (truck/trailer)
- Insurance

## Revenue Model
- Driveways: $150-300
- House exteriors: $200-500
- Decks/patios: $150-350
- Commercial: $300-800 per visit
- Add-on services increase ticket size

## Getting Started
1. Buy or lease quality equipment
2. Get business insurance
3. Practice on your own and friends' homes
4. Before/after photos for marketing
5. Door-to-door in neighborhoods
6. Google Local Services ads
7. Seasonal: Spring/summer busy, plan for winter
