# Podcast Production Service

**Category:** Digital Service
**Startup Cost:** Low ($100-500)
**Time to Profit:** 2-4 months
**Difficulty:** Medium

## Description
Offer podcast editing and production services to busy podcasters who want to focus on content.

## Services to Offer
- Audio editing and cleanup
- Show notes and transcription
- Episode artwork
- Podcast launch setup
- Content repurposing (clips for social)
- Guest scheduling and research

## Tools Needed
- DAW software (Adobe Audition, Audacity)
- Noise reduction plugins
- Transcription service (Otter.ai, Rev)
- Project management (Notion, Trello)

## Revenue Model
- Per episode editing: $50-200
- Package deals: $400-800/month (4-8 episodes)
- Launch packages: $500-2,000
- Content repurposing: $100-300/episode

## Getting Started
1. Learn editing thoroughly (practice on free podcasts)
2. Create before/after samples
3. Find podcasters in Facebook groups, Reddit
4. Offer free trial episode to new clients
5. Network with podcast guests
6. Upsell additional services over time
