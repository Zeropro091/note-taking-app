# Niche Web Design Agency

**Category:** Digital Service
**Startup Cost:** Low ($100-500)
**Time to Profit:** 2-4 months
**Difficulty:** Medium

## Description
Build websites for a specific industry. Niche focus leads to expertise and higher rates.

## Profitable Niches
- Restaurants (ordering systems)
- Real estate (agent sites)
- Medical/dental practices
- Local service businesses
- E-commerce brands
- Personal branding (coaches, consultants)

## Revenue Model
- Simple sites: $1,000-3,000
- Complex sites: $5,000-15,000
- Monthly retainers: $200-500/month
- E-commerce: $3,000-10,000

## Getting Started
1. Learn WordPress/Webflow
2. Build 2-3 portfolio sites (fake if needed)
3. Pick ONE niche
4. Cold email businesses with bad sites
5. Offer specific improvements, not just "redesign"
6. Add maintenance retainers for recurring revenue

## Scaling Tips
1. Create templates for your niche
2. Hire freelancers for overflow
3. Add hosting/marketing services
4. Build relationships with complementary services
