# Professional Home Organization

**Category:** Local Service
**Startup Cost:** Very Low ($0-200)
**Time to Profit:** Immediate
**Difficulty:** Low

## Description
Help people declutter and organize their homes. Great for organization enthusiasts.

## Services to Offer
- Room-by-room organization
- Decluttering guidance
- Closet organization
- Kitchen organization
- Garage organization
- Moving/unpacking help
- Estate organization

## Revenue Model
- Per room: $200-500
- Per day: $400-800
- Whole home packages: $1,500-5,000
- Maintenance visits: $100-200

## Getting Started
1. Practice on friends/family homes
2. Before/after photos for portfolio
3. Decide if you sell products (bins, labels)
4. Market on Instagram (visual business)
5. Partner with realtors, movers
6. Consider "organization + products" package

## Add-On Revenue
- Sell organization products (markup)
- Affiliate with Container Store, etc.
- Digital guides and checklists
- Virtual organizing consultations
