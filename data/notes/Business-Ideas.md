### Local Business Optimization (Indonesia)

**The Problem**: International pricing doesn't work in Indonesia

**Solution**:
- Adjust pricing for local purchasing power
- Focus on high-volume, lower-margin
- Build for local payment ecosystems (GoPay, OVO, Dana)
- Use local language and cultural context

**See Also**: [[Business-Entities]]
