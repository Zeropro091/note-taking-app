# Stock Photography Business

**Category:** Creative
**Startup Cost:** Medium ($1,000-5,000 for equipment)
**Time to Profit:** 6-12 months
**Difficulty:** Medium

## Description
Sell high-quality stock photos through marketplaces and directly to businesses.

## In-Demand Categories
- Business and office settings
- Healthcare and medical
- Technology and AI
- Diverse lifestyle images
- Food and drink
- Nature and travel

## Revenue Streams
- Stock marketplaces (Shutterstock, Adobe Stock, Getty)
- Direct sales to businesses
- Print-on-demand products
- Photography services (upsell)

## Pricing Guide
- Stock sites: $0.25-100 per download (you get 20-50%)
- Direct licensing: $50-500 per image
- Custom shoots: $500-5,000 per day

## Getting Started
1. Learn photography fundamentals
2. Invest in decent camera gear
3. Upload to multiple marketplaces
4. Focus on underserved niches
5. Consistent uploads (quantity + quality)
6. Keyword research for discoverability
7. Build direct client relationships
