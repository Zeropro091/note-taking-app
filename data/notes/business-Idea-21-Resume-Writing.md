# Resume Writing Service

**Category:** Career Services
**Startup Cost:** Very Low ($0-100)
**Time to Profit:** 1-2 months
**Difficulty:** Low

## Description
Help job seekers create professional resumes, cover letters, and LinkedIn profiles.

## Services to Offer
- Resume writing/editing
- Cover letter writing
- LinkedIn profile optimization
- Interview preparation
- Career coaching
- Salary negotiation help

## Revenue Model
- Entry-level resumes: $100-200
- Professional resumes: $200-400
- Executive resumes: $400-800
- Packages (resume + cover letter + LinkedIn): $300-700
- Rush service: 50% premium

## Getting Started
1. Study resume best practices
2. Create before/after samples
3. Offer free reviews to build testimonials
4. Network on LinkedIn
5. Partner with career services, universities
6. Create templates for passive income

## Scaling Tips
1. Specialize in an industry
2. Create questionnaire to streamline
3. Hire contract writers
4. Build an agency
5. Add career coaching (higher rates)
