# Online Store Flipping

**Category:** E-commerce
**Startup Cost:** Medium ($1,000-10,000)
**Time to Profit:** 6-12 months
**Difficulty:** High

## Description
Buy undervalued online businesses, improve them, and sell for profit. Or build from scratch and sell.

## Types of Sites
- Content sites (ad revenue)
- E-commerce (dropshipping, FBA)
- SaaS micro-products
- FBA Amazon businesses
- Newsletter/mailing lists

## Where to Buy/Sell
- Empire Flippers (higher end)
- Flippa (mixed quality)
- MicroAcquire (tech focused)
- Facebook groups (deals)

## Value Multipliers
- Content sites: 20-40x monthly profit
- E-commerce: 2-4x annual profit
- SaaS: 3-6x annual revenue
- Newsletters: $10-20 per subscriber

## Strategy
1. Start by building a site from scratch
2. Learn SEO, content, monetization
3. Sell first site for experience
4. Reinvest profits into buying
5. Fix and flip for profit

## Risks
- Algorithm changes
- Competition
- Due diligence is critical
- Cash needed for inventory/fees
