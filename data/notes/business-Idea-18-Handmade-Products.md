# Handmade Products Business

**Category:** E-commerce
**Startup Cost:** Low ($100-500)
**Time to Profit:** 2-4 months
**Difficulty:** Medium

## Description
Sell handmade crafts on Etsy, local markets, and your own site.

## Profitable Categories
- Jewelry (necklaces, earrings)
- Home decor (wreaths, signs)
- Candles and soaps
- Clothing and accessories
- Pet accessories
- Kids toys and clothes
- Personalized gifts

## Sales Channels
- Etsy (built-in traffic)
- Shopify (own brand)
- Local craft fairs and markets
- Instagram/TikTok (organic)
- Wholesale to local shops

## Revenue Model
- Price: Materials × 3-4
- Start with 10-20 SKUs
- Seasonal items boost sales
- Custom orders = premium pricing

## Getting Started
1. Master one product category
2. Perfect your craft
3. Beautiful photography is essential
4. Start on Etsy for traffic
5. Build email list from day one
6. Scale with systems and possibly hiring
