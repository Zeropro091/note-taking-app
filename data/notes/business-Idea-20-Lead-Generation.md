# Local Lead Generation

**Category:** Digital Service
**Startup Cost:** Low ($100-500)
**Time to Profit:** 3-6 months
**Difficulty:** Medium

## Description
Build websites that rank for local service keywords and sell the leads to local businesses.

## Profitable Niches
- Roofing, plumbing, HVAC
- Tree service, landscaping
- Concrete, fencing
- Carpet cleaning, maid service
- Pest control
- Electricians

## How It Works
1. Build lead gen site for city + service
2. Rank organically or run ads
3. Capture leads (form, phone)
4. Sell leads to local businesses
5. Or rent the site monthly

## Revenue Model
- Per lead: $20-100 depending on value
- Site rental: $500-2,000/month
- Or sell whole site: 10-30x monthly profit

## Getting Started
1. Pick high-value service niche
2. Build simple lead gen site
3. SEO or local service ads
4. Call businesses to sell leads
5. Track results carefully
6. Scale across cities and services

## Challenges
- SEO takes time (6-12 months)
- Ad costs fluctuate
- Client relationships
- Competition in good niches
