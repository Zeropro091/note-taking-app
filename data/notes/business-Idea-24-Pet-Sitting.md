# Pet Sitting and Dog Walking

**Category:** Local Service
**Startup Cost:** Very Low ($0-100)
**Time to Profit:** Immediate
**Difficulty:** Low

## Description
Pet care services including dog walking, pet sitting, and boarding.

## Services to Offer
- Dog walking (30-60 min)
- Pet sitting (drop-in visits)
- Overnight pet sitting in your home
- Pet boarding (your home)
- Pet taxi (vet visits)
- Administering medication

## Revenue Model
- Dog walking: $15-30 per walk
- Drop-in visits: $15-25 per visit
- Overnight sitting: $50-80 per night
- Boarding: $40-60 per night
- Monthly packages for regulars

## Getting Started
1. Bonded and insured (trust is key)
2. Apps: Rover, Wag (for clients)
3. Facebook local community groups
4. Nextdoor neighborhood app
5. Start with friends/family for referrals
6. Get reviews immediately
7. Background check badge helps

## Scaling
1. Hire reliable walkers/sitters
2. Take management fee (20-30%)
3. Focus on booking and quality
4. Add premium services (grooming, training)
