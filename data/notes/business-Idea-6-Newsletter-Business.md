# Paid Newsletter Business

**Category:** Media
**Startup Cost:** Very Low ($0-100)
**Time to Profit:** 3-12 months
**Difficulty:** Medium

## Description
Build an audience around a specific topic and monetize through paid subscriptions.

## Profitable Niches
- Investing tips (stocks, crypto)
- Industry news (tech, AI, SaaS)
- Career advice (specific field)
- Health and wellness
- Gaming and entertainment
- Local news and events

## Revenue Streams
- Paid subscriptions ($5-50/month)
- Sponsorships ($500-5,000 per issue)
- Affiliate products
- Premium courses/consulting

## Platforms
- Substack (easiest start)
- Beehiiv (better growth tools)
- Ghost (self-hosted)
- ConvertKit (email + community)

## Getting Started
1. Pick niche with passionate audience
2. Commit to consistent publishing (weekly)
3. Offer free value, upgrade to paid
4. Grow audience (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, SEO)
5. Launch paid tier at 1,000+ free subscribers
6. Target: 100 paid at $10/month = $1,000/mo
