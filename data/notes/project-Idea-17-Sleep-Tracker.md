# Sleep Tracker

**Category:** Health
**Effort:** Medium
**Monetization:** Freemium

## Description
Track sleep patterns with smart alarm, sleep sounds, and AI-powered sleep improvement suggestions.

## Key Features
- Sleep tracking (movement/sound or wearable sync)
- Smart alarm (wake during light sleep)
- Sleep sound library (white noise, nature)
- Sleep goal tracking
- Caffeine/alcohol tracking (effects on sleep)
- Bedtime reminders
- Sleep quality score
- Morning energy log
- Integration with Apple Health/Google Fit

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React Native (accelerometer/audio)
- Backend: Python/Node.js
- AI: Simple sleep quality model
- Wearable: Apple HealthKit, Google Fit
- Audio: Custom sound loops

## Revenue Model
- Free: Basic tracking
- Premium: $5.99/month - Smart alarm, sleep sounds, insights
- Lifetime: $39.99
