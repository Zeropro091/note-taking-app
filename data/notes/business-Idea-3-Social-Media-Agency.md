# Niche Social Media Agency

**Category:** Digital Marketing
**Startup Cost:** Low ($100-500)
**Time to Profit:** 2-4 months
**Difficulty:** Medium

## Description
Social media management agency focused on a specific industry (restaurants, real estate, healthcare, local businesses).

## Services to Offer
- Content creation and scheduling
- Community management
- Paid ad management
- Analytics and reporting
- Influencer outreach
- Strategy consulting

## Pick a Niche
- Restaurants/bars (visual-heavy, local SEO)
- Real estate agents (listing promotion)
- Healthcare (HIPAA-compliant marketing)
- Local retail (community-focused)
- B2B SaaS (LinkedIn-focused)

## Revenue Model
- Content only: $500-1,500/month
- Full management: $2,000-5,000/month
- Ad management: 15-20% of ad spend
- One-time setups: $1,000-3,000

## Getting Started
1. Choose ONE niche to start
2. Create portfolio with mock/sample content
3. Offer free/cheap service to 2-3 clients for testimonials
4. Case studies → outreach to similar businesses
5. Network at local business events
