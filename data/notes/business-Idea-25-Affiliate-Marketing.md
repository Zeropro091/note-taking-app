# Affiliate Marketing Business

**Category:** Passive Income
**Startup Cost:** Low ($100-500)
**Time to Profit:** 6-12 months
**Difficulty:** Medium

## Description
Build niche websites and content that drives traffic to affiliate products for commission.

## Profitable Niches
- Software and SaaS reviews
- Product comparisons
- How-to guides with product recommendations
- Deal and coupon sites
- Resource pages

## Affiliate Programs
- Amazon Associates (4-10%)
- Software (20-50% recurring)
- Courses (30-50%)
- Financial products (high payout)
- Hostinger/Bluehost ($65-130 per signup)

## Revenue Model
- Traffic × Conversion rate × Commission
- Goal: 1,000+ visitors/day
- Email list = more sales per visitor
- SEO traffic = passive income

## Getting Started
1. Pick a niche you know
2. Build authority site with helpful content
3. Join affiliate programs
4. Focus on SEO and email list
5. Create comparison content (buys now)
6. Track everything and optimize

## Long-Term Strategy
1. Diversify traffic sources
2. Build multiple sites
3. Create your own product (keep 100%)
4. Sell sites for lump sum
