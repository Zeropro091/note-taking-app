# Lead Generation Systems - Fill Your Pipeline

## The Lead Generation Equation

**Leads = Traffic × Conversion Rate × Offer Quality**

Focus on all three, not just one.

---

## Inbound Lead Sources

### 1. Content Marketing
**Blog posts that solve specific problems**

```
Title Formula:
"How to [solve problem] without [pain point]"
"5 [Industry] Terms You're Using Wrong"
"The Complete Guide to [Topic] for [Audience]"
```

**Distribution**:
- Your blog/website
- LinkedIn (for B2B)
- Medium (for tech audience)
- Instagram/TikTok (for consumers)

**CTA in every piece**: "Want help implementing this? Let's talk."

### 2. Social Media Presence
**Platform strategy for Indonesia** (see [[Local-Business-Optimization]]):

| Platform | Best For | Content Type |
|----------|----------|--------------|
| Instagram | Visual brands | Behind scenes, results, lifestyle |
| TikTok | Viral reach | Quick tips, trends, entertainment |
| LinkedIn | B2B professionals | Insights, case studies, thought leadership |
| YouTube | Deep tutorials | How-to, explanations, demos |

**Post formula**:
```
Hook (first 3 seconds) → Problem → Solution → CTA

Example:
"Stop losing 30% of your customers to competitors.
Here's the 3-Step fix used by top restaurants.
[explain steps]
Want help implementing? DM me 'RETAIN'."
```

### 3. SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
**Rank for problems, not your company name.**

**Keyword research**:
- What do your ideal customers type into Google?
- "How to [solve problem]"
- "Best [solution] for [industry]"
- "[Industry] software/tools/services"

**On-page SEO**:
- Title tags with benefit
- Meta descriptions with CTA
- URL structure (clean, readable)
- Internal linking

**See**: [[Landing-Page-Systems]]

### 4. Referrals
**The highest quality lead source.**

**Systematize referrals**:
```
1. Deliver exceptional work (obviously)
2. Ask at the right time: "Do you know anyone else who [X]?"
3. Make it easy: Referral link, intro email template
4. Say thank you: Gift card, handwritten note, shout-out
```

**Referral script**:
```
"Hey [Name], we're wrapping up the project
and I'm really proud of how it turned out.

If you know anyone else who might benefit from [service],
I'd appreciate the introduction.

I take care of my network, so they'll be in good hands."
```

## Outbound Lead Generation

### 5. Cold Email
**Personalized, relevant, brief.**

**Template**:
```
Subject: [Specific problem] at [Company]

Hi [Name],

I saw that you're [specific observation]
and noticed you might be struggling with [problem].

We helped [similar company] solve this by [method],
resulting in [specific outcome].

Would you be open to a 15-min call to discuss
if this could work for [Company]?

No pressure—just sharing an idea.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Key**: Research before reaching out. Not spam.

### 6. Strategic Partnerships
**Find businesses serving the same customers, non-competitively.**

**Example for web services**:
- Graphic designers (need development for their designs)
- Marketing agencies (need tech implementation)
- Accountants (clients asking for tech help)
- Print shops (clients need websites)

**Partnership offer**:
```
"I'll refer my web clients to you for [your service].
In exchange, refer your [your service] clients to me for web.

We can even create a package deal.
Want to test it with one client each?"
```

### 7. Networking Events
**But not random networking.**

**Targeted approach**:
1. Research attendees beforehand
2. Pick 3-5 specific people to meet
3. Have thoughtful questions prepared
4. Follow up within 24 hours

**Good questions**:
- "What's the biggest challenge you're facing right now?"
- "What kind of introductions would be most valuable to you?"
- "How do you like to help your clients?"

**Bad questions**:
- "So what do you do?" (You should know this already)
- "How can I help you?" (Too vague)

Give value in exchange for contact info.

## Lead Capture Systems

### On Your Website
**Every page should have a CTA.**

**Above the fold**: Primary CTA
```
"Need help with [service]?
Chat with us on WhatsApp →"
```

**Below content**: Secondary CTA
```
"Not ready yet?
Get our free [resource] →"
```

**Exit intent popup**:
```
"Before you go, want our free [lead magnet]?"
```

### Landing Page Structure
See [[Landing-Page-Systems]] for complete guide.

**Essential elements**:
- Clear headline (benefit, not feature)
- Social proof (testimonials, logos)
- Single CTA (no confusion)
- Mobile-optimized (90%+ Indonesian users)
- Fast loading (< 3 seconds)

## Lead Qualification

### The IDEAL Profile

For [[Business-Entities]] services:

**Ideal client profile**:
- Small to medium business (10-100 employees)
- Revenue: IDR 500M - 50B/year
- Already has basic website/presence
- Looking to grow
- Decision maker accessible

**Red flags**:
- No website yet (educate first)
- Budget under IDR 1M/year (too small)
- Government entity (slow, bureaucratic)
- "Just want to see what you offer" (not serious)

## Lead Nurturing Sequence

### Email/WhatsApp Drip Campaign

**Day 0**: Deliver the lead magnet
```
"Here's your [resource]!
As promised: [link]

While you look at that, I wanted to share:
[one quick tip related to resource]"

Any questions, just hit reply!"
```

**Day 2**: Add value
```
"I noticed a lot of people who downloaded [resource]
also ask about [related topic].

Here's a quick video explanation:
[link to content]"
```

**Day 5**: Soft sell
```
"How are you finding [resource]?

If you're ready to implement but need help,
I have 2 spots open this month for [service].

No pressure—just wanted you to know."
```

**Day 14**: Check in
```
"Hey, just checking in on [resource].

Did you have any questions?

Also, I just published [new content]
that you might find helpful: [link]"
```

**Day 30**: Re-engage
```
"It's been a month!

I wanted to share a case study:
[How client achieved X result]

If you're ever ready to take the next step,
you know where to find me."
```
