# Niche Dropshipping Store

**Category:** E-commerce
**Startup Cost:** Low ($200-500)
**Time to Profit:** 3-6 months
**Difficulty:** Medium

## Description
E-commerce store selling curated products in a specific niche, with fulfillment handled by suppliers.

## Profitable Niches
- Pet products (eco-friendly, smart devices)
- Home office ergonomics
- Sustainable kitchen products
- Fitness accessories
- Gaming accessories

## What You Need
- Shopify or WooCommerce store
- Supplier relationships (AliExpress, US suppliers)
- Brand identity and content
- Marketing budget (ads, influencers)

## Revenue Model
- Product markup: 2-4x cost
- Average order value: $50-150
- Target margin: 30-50%
- Email/SMS marketing for repeat purchases

## Getting Started
1. Research niche competition and demand
2. Find reliable suppliers with fast shipping
3. Build brand (not just a generic store)
4. Start with 10-20 winning products
5. Use TikTok/Instagram Reels for organic traffic
6. Test ads with small budget ($20-50/day)
