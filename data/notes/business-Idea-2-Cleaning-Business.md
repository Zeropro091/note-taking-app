# Eco-Friendly Cleaning Service

**Category:** Local Service
**Startup Cost:** Low ($500-2,000)
**Time to Profit:** 1-2 months
**Difficulty:** Low

## Description
Professional cleaning service using only eco-friendly, non-toxic products. Target health-conscious homeowners and businesses.

## Services to Offer
- Residential cleaning (regular, deep clean, move-in/out)
- Commercial cleaning (offices, retail)
- Post-construction cleaning
- Green products only
- Recyclable packaging

## Target Markets
- Families with children/pets
- Elderly clients
- Eco-conscious businesses
- Healthcare facilities
- Real estate agents (for home staging)

## Revenue Model
- Residential: $100-200 per cleaning
- Commercial: $200-500 per visit
- Monthly contracts: $400-1,200/month
- Deep cleaning: $250-500

## Getting Started
1. Research eco-friendly products
2. Get insurance and bonding
3. Create simple website with booking
4. Market on Nextdoor, Facebook local groups
5. Offer discount for first-time customers
