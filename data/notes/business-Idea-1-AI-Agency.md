# AI Automation Agency

**Category:** B2B Service
**Startup Cost:** Low ($500-2,000)
**Time to Profit:** 1-3 months
**Difficulty:** Medium

## Description
Help businesses implement AI automation to save time and reduce costs. Focus on specific use cases like customer service, content creation, or data entry.

## Services to Offer
- Chatbot implementation (customer service, lead qualification)
- AI content generation (blogs, social media, emails)
- Workflow automation (Zapier, Make, n8n)
- Document processing and data extraction
- AI training for teams

## Target Clients
- Real estate agents (property descriptions, follow-ups)
- E-commerce stores (product descriptions, customer support)
- Law firms (document review, contract analysis)
- Marketing agencies (content at scale)

## Revenue Model
- Project-based: $2,000-10,000 per implementation
- Monthly retainers: $1,000-5,000/month for ongoing support
- Consulting: $150-300/hour

## Getting Started
1. Learn automation tools (Zapier, Make, OpenAI API)
2. Create case studies with personal projects
3. Cold email businesses with specific automation ideas
4. Network on LinkedIn
