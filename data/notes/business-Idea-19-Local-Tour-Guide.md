# Local Tour Guide Business

**Category:** Local/Tourism
**Startup Cost:** Very Low ($0-200)
**Time to Profit:** 1-3 months
**Difficulty:** Low

## Description
Create unique walking tours or experiences in your city for tourists and locals.

## Tour Ideas
- Historical walking tours
- Food and drink tours
- Ghost/haunted tours
- Street art tours
- Architecture tours
- Niche interests (music, movies, books)
- Local hidden gems

## Revenue Model
- Group tours: $25-50 per person
- Private tours: $150-400 per group
- Duration: 2-3 hours typical
- Tips often add 15-20%

## Platforms
- Airbnb Experiences
- Viator/TripAdvisor
- GetYourGuide
- Direct booking (website)

## Getting Started
1. Research your city's unique stories
2. Plan a 2-3 hour route
3. Practice with friends/family
4. List on multiple platforms
5. Encourage reviews (critical for ranking)
6. Add more tours as you grow
7. Partner with local businesses (commission)
